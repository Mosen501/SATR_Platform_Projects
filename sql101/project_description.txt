Using what you have learned during this course, apply the following: Assume there is a school named Excellence Secondary School, and we want to create a centralized database to include all information about teachers, students, and subjects, bearing in mind that the following information must be stored for each student (student serial number, student name, date of birth, gender, date of enrollment, student email, academic level, track, cumulative GPA), and for teachers the following information (teacher serial number, teacher name, date of birth, gender, teacher email, office number), and for subjects the following information (subject serial number, subject name).

Requirements:

Use comments to explain the commands.

Create the database.

Create the tables.

Display the tables available in the database.

Enter information for at least 30 students.

Enter information for at least 10 teachers.

Enter information for at least 6 subjects.

Display the contents of all tables.

Display the contents of the students table with the rows ordered ascending by student name.

Display the contents of the students table while giving an alias to the “student name” field to shorten the column name or make it more meaningful.

Modify the data.

Modify one student’s record and change the student’s email.

Modify one teacher’s record and change the teacher’s office number.

Modify the tables.

Rename one of the tables.

Notes:

The serial number is the ID.

Gender is F or M.

The academic level consists of six levels (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6).

The track is scientific or humanities.

The cumulative GPA is out of 100.
